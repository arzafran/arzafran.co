Thoughts on habituation

Habituation... the word is pretty self-explaining, to make a habit from something, but my focus is on that something, it was something that once required all of our focus, our whole universe was around something that now is purely a reflex, an automated task.

Our brains are amazing machines but they can only focus on one thing at a time, and by habituating stuff they're freeing up space to keep learning new things that require our whole concentration at least for a period of time, until it becomes a habit. But, I don't want my brain to make a habit from everything, and by feeding my creativity, questioning everything, and always trying to solve problems by disruptive thinking is how I try to deny that.

This is a proof of concept. Just a way to say stuff, anything, something. :)
